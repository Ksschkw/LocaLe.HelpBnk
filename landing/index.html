How it works

3 steps. Zero stress.

Built for the way local economies actually work — fast, community-driven, cash-first in spirit but safe in practice.

01
📋

Book a Job

Post what you need — laundry, repair, cleaning, errands. Pick from nearby verified providers. Set your price.

→
02
🔒

Funds Secured

Transfer or card payment goes into escrow. Provider is notified and starts the job. No one touches the money yet.

→
03
📷

QR Release

Satisfied? Scan the provider's QR code. Payment releases instantly — under 5 minutes. Provider gets paid via transfer.

💡
The QR code works even with weak network. No "my internet was bad" excuses. And yes — the code is one-time use, tied to your specific job only.
