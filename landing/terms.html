LocaLe: Core Marketplace & Escrow Terms (v1.0)

1. The Escrow Rule (The "Vault" Policy)

  • The Hold: When a Buyer pays for a job, LocaLe holds 100% of the funds securely in escrow.
  • The Release: Funds are only released to the Provider when the Buyer provides the 6-digit Verification Code or scans the QR Code.
  • Automatic Release: If a Provider marks a job as "Completed" and the Buyer does not "Release" or "Dispute" within 24 hours, the system may trigger an automatic release after internal review.

2. The 30/70 "Commitment" Split

  • To prevent "no-shows," the 30% Deposit is non-refundable if the Provider arrives at the location and the Buyer cancels without valid cause.
  • The 70% Balance is strictly tied to the "Job Done" checklist.

3. Dispute & Refund Policy

  • The "Hold" Trigger: If a Buyer is unhappy, they must tap "Report Issue" instead of sharing the code. This freezes the funds instantly.
  • Evidence: Both parties must provide photo/video proof within 12 hours of a dispute being opened.
  • Refunds: If the Provider fails to deliver the "Job Done" checklist, a full or partial refund is issued to the Buyer’s LocaLe wallet/original payment method.

4. Service Provider Agreement

  • Independent Contractor: Providers are not employees of LocaLe. They are independent neighborhood entrepreneurs.
  • The "Off-Platform" Ban: Any Provider caught asking a Buyer to "pay cash directly" to bypass the escrow for a LocaLe-initiated job will be permanently banned. This protects the integrity of the "Trust Vault."

5. Data & Identity Policy

  • Identity: Basic "Social Vouching" (Phone + Neighborhood) is required for all jobs. Valid Government-issued ID is mandatory for high-value jobs (exceeding the regional platform threshold).
  • Privacy: We only share the Provider's first name and rating with the Buyer until the job is accepted. Precise locations are only revealed once the deposit is secured.
