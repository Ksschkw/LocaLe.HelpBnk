Escrow Flow Overview

The LocaLe escrow flow ensures trust between buyers and service providers. Money is held until the buyer verifies job completion via QR scan. The provider gets paid instantly after verification.

📋 Booking → 🔒 Fund & Hold → ✅ Funds Secured → 📱 QR Release → 📷 Buyer Scans → ✓ Confirm → 💚 Payment Done
Release authorization: Buyer scans Provider's QR, then confirms release. Provider gets paid in under 5 minutes.
Edge cases handled: Cancellation (full refund before job start) and Dispute (funds held, support reviews within 24h).

Step 1: Choose a Service

1
Home Screen — Service Selection
User lands on the home screen and sees available services: Laundry, Room Cleaning, Errand/Delivery, Phone Repair, Generator Repair. Each card shows price range and a trust indicator. A banner reminds users to "Add to Home Screen" for PWA experience.
[Screenshot: Home page — index.html]
2
Job Details + Price Selection
User enters job details: auto-detected location, description, preferred time, and budget (₦3,000 / ₦5,000 / ₦10,000 or custom). Clear messaging: "This amount will be held in escrow."
[Screenshot: Job page — job.html]

Step 2: Choose Provider & Fund Escrow

3
Select a Provider
2–3 verified provider cards with: name, distance, rating, review count, and "Vouched by [neighbor name]". Social proof builds trust before the financial step.
[Screenshot: Provider selection — provider.html]
4
Fund & Hold (Mock Payment)
Key trust moment. Big banner: "Your money is held safely until the job is confirmed done." Default: Bank Transfer (most popular among students). Account details shown with copy buttons. Alternative: Card, USSD. Small print: "Provider cannot receive payment until you scan to release."
[Screenshot: Checkout — checkout.html]

Step 3: Funds Secured Status

5
"Funds Secured" — Clear Trust Message
Big green badge: "Payment is Secured. Provider has been notified." Visual timeline shows progress: Booked → Funds Secured → In Progress → Job Done → Release. Shows escrow reference number (e.g., LCL-4821). Messaging: "Provider gets paid instantly after you verify completion with a QR scan." Note: "Code works even with weak network."
[Screenshot: Funds Secured — status.html]
Why this screen matters: This is the #1 trust moment. Users need to believe their money is safe and understand the release mechanism. In testing, we measure whether users feel reassured or still anxious at this point.

Step 4: QR Code Release

6
Provider Shows QR Code
Provider's screen: Shows a one-time QR code + 6-digit backup code tied to this specific job. Message: "Customer scans this QR after the job is complete." QR expires in 10 minutes. Amount to receive shown clearly. Payout guarantee: "Payment released in under 5 minutes."
[Screenshot: Provider QR — provider-qr.html]
7
Buyer Scans QR Code
Buyer's screen: Camera viewfinder with scanning animation. Fallback: 6-digit backup code entry. The code is verified client-side (in the mock). If valid → proceeds to confirmation. If invalid → "Invalid code" error with retry.
[Screenshot: QR Scan — scan.html]

Step 5: Confirm Release & Receipt

8
Confirm Release
Final confirmation: shows provider name, amount, and fee breakdown. Required checkbox: "I confirm the job is complete and I'm satisfied." Warning: "After release, funds are sent instantly. This cannot be reversed." The release button only activates after checkbox is checked.
[Screenshot: Confirm Release — confirm.html]
9
Payment Released — Receipt
Success screen with animated checkmark. Full receipt: Job ID, provider, amount, fee, total, time, date. Status: "Complete". "Provider gets paid in under 5 minutes." Optional: star rating for the provider. "Done — Back to Home" CTA.
[Screenshot: Receipt — receipt.html]

Edge Cases: Cancel & Dispute

—
Cancellation (Job Not Started)
If job hasn't started, buyer can cancel and get a full refund (including security fee). Refund timeline shown: 1–2 hours. Clear messaging that no money is lost.
[Screenshot: Cancellation — cancel.html]
—
Dispute (Problem Reported)
If there's a problem: funds are held, buyer describes the issue, can upload photos. Support reviews within 24 hours. Resolution options: full refund, partial release, or full release. "Your money is 100% protected."
[Screenshot: Dispute — dispute.html]
